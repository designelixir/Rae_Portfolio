West Flanders Brewing Co.

A release poster for West Flanders Brewing Company's new "Brut-ster" IPA. This beer was named in honor of our very own "brewster" (female brewing scientist) Erin, who epitomizes bad-ass women. "Traffic" Album Artwork

Killa Nilla

Given complete creative freedom, I developed two album artworks for Dubstep artist and friend Killa Nilla. Driven by the hypnotic and space-inspired style of Dubstep culture, I used Adobe Draw on the iPad to develop two abstract works to showcase his tracks.

Renu Cosmetics Logo

Renu Cosmetics is a small, natural brand of beauty products designed by Paige Johns out of Salt Lake City, Utah. Her career as an esthetician has inspired her to launch her own line of hand-crafted natural beauty products. In order to promote a minimalist and natural look, I provided paige with several options. Packaging was meant to be home-made looking and authentic.

Menu Rework

West Flanders Brewing Co.

Create a menu that clearly organizes each food category, as well as make a la carte options both easier to find, and more clearly explain the additional price charge for ordering them. Other design goals included reducing page size, improving menu flow by organizing sections, and incorporating our brand. I started by drawing some digital elements, hops, as decoration for the menu.

Membership Brochure

CiviCO

Contracted work for the rebranding of then Quarterly Forum to Civico. Given brand standards, I was assigned to recreate the information in Quarterly Forum's previous membership brochure following new typefaces, color standards and brand attributes while reducing the number of pages in half.

Brochure Pages

Self Portrait

Using ten photos, I created a self-portrait montage that exhibited an emotion for a photography class. All photos used were captured during a month long backpacking trip of the Colorado Trail. After creating a dozen layers, I traced each layer with my mouse to give a hand-drawn, euphoric vibe. I hoped to express how I feel while in Mother Nature - specifically during my backpacking trip.
